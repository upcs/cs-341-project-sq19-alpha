Tier One 101 is a post-disaster preparation site created to provide you with the information you need to keep you and your loved ones safe. A tier one critical facility is a location recognized by the Portland Bureau of Emergency Management for having great importance in a post-disaster situation. This site will provide you with important "tips & tricks" for various types of disasters and generate a route to your nearest tier one facility. You will also find a list of phone numbers to contact for any other questions or concerns.
