The following page contains information about what to do incase different natural disasters occur. This is meant to be used prior to any disaster, incase of a disaster follow protocol, if you are unsure what to do then find an official who may help you.

Earthquake

1.Choose a safe place in every room, best to get under a sturdy piece of furniture.
2.During the event of an earthquake, drop, cover and hold on. Stay indoors until the shaking stops.
3.Stay away from windows.
4.expect aftershocks!

Tsunami

1.Go as high and far as you can.
2. A tsunami warning: a tsunami may have been generated and could be close to your area.
3. A tsunami watch: tsunami has not been verified but could exist.
4.Avoid downed power lines and get to higher ground.

Tornado

1.Go to windowless interior room on lowest level of house.
2. If there is no basement, go to an inner hallway or an inner smaller room, like a bathroom or closet.
3. Get away from the windows. Go to the center of the room.
4. Get under a piece of sturdy furniture.

Flooding

1.Climb to safety immediately, get out of low aread subject to flooding.
2. If driving don't go through flooded roadways.
3. Assemble your disaster supplies. Be prepared to evacuate, identify places to go.
4. Fill your car's gas tank. And if you're told to leave, do so quickly!

Wildfire

IF YOU ARE UNDER A WILDFIRE WARNING:
1. Leave if told to do so.
2. If trapped, called 9-1-1.
3. Listen for emergency information and alert.
4. Use N95 masks to keep particles out of the air you breathe

Zombies

1.Clear the room.
2.Never turn your back on the enemy.
3.Zombies are the least of your worries.
4.Choose your weapons wisely.
5. Windows are not your friend. Suit up.
